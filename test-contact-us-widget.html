üìã How to Use the Contact Us Widget

  1. Login to your account and navigate to the Widgets section
  2. Find the Contact Us widget and click "Manage Widget"
  3. Customize your form:
    • Change title and subtitle
    • Add/remove/reorder form fields
    • Customize colors to match your brand
    • Set field requirements (required/optional)
  4. Copy the embed code from the management page
  5. Paste it into your website where you want the contact form to appear
  6. View messages in the "Messages" tab of the widget management page
